Biography

Junbo Zhang is currently a Data Scientist at JD Finance Group. He is leading AI Platform Division of Urban Computing Business Unit, JD Finance Group. His research interests include deep learning, big data analytics, large-scale machine learning, and spatio-temporal data mining.

He has published over 30 research papers (e.g., AI, IEEE TKDE, KDD, AAAI, IJCAI) in refereed journals and conferences, among which one paper was selected as the ESI Hot Paper, two as the ESI Highly Cited Paper, and two as the Best Paper Award. He co-authored the book “The Principles and Methodologies of Big Data Mining—-From the Perspectives of Granular Computing and Rough Sets”. Dr. Zhang received the ACM Chengdu Doctoral Dissertation Award in 2016, the Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAAI) Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Nomination Award in 2016, the Si Shi Yang Hua Medal (Top 1⁄1000) of SWJTU in 2012, and the Outstanding Ph.D. Graduate of Sichuan Province in 2013. He is a member of IEEE, ACM, CAAI and China Computer Federation.
